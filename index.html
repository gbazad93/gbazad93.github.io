Who I Am

My Story

I'm a Data & AI Engineer and Tech Lead at Agtegra Cooperative in South Dakota, where I work at the frontier of AI, automation, and agriculture. My journey started with software engineering, took me through graduate research in machine learning and precision agriculture at SDSU, and now drives enterprise AI transformation in the American heartland.

I specialize in taking AI from whiteboard to production — designing intelligent systems, deploying ML models, building enterprise apps, and developing AI-powered solutions that make knowledge instantly accessible to teams. I believe great engineering is as much about the people it serves as the code it runs.

Beyond work, I'm an active member of the Python Software Foundation, attend PyCon annually, and maintain open-source projects on GitHub. When I'm not in front of a terminal, you'll find me at Aberdeen New Life Fellowship Church playing acoustic guitar on the worship team, or out fishing on a South Dakota lake.
