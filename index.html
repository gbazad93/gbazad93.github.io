Who I Am

My Story

I'm a Data & AI Engineer and Tech Lead at Agtegra Cooperative in South Dakota, where I work at the frontier of AI, automation, and agriculture. My journey started with software engineering in Iran, took me through graduate research in machine learning and precision agriculture at SDSU, and now drives enterprise transformation in the American heartland.

I specialize in taking AI from whiteboard to production — designing Airflow pipelines, deploying ML models, building enterprise apps, and developing RAG-based systems that make intelligent search a reality for teams. I believe great engineering is as much about the people it serves as the code it runs.

Beyond work, I'm an active member of the Python Software Foundation, attend PyCon annually, and maintain open-source projects on GitHub. When I'm not in front of a terminal, you'll find me at Aberdeen New Life Fellowship Church playing acoustic guitar on the worship team, or out fishing on a South Dakota lake.
